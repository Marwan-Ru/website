Marwan Ait Addi-Russier

I'm a First year PhD candidate at LIRIS Laboratory in Lyon.

I am part of the TEATIME project. My work focuses on compressing and visualising n-dimensional heritage data, hoping to discover new ways to link and analyse. The Notre-Dame de Paris dataset is my main case study. (I also play the Trombone !)
